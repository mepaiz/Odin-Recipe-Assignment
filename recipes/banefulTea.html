Baneful Tea

A tea to harm your enemies

A dangerous drink, best prepared with caution and administered with gravitas. Allow for 3-5 hours for effects to kick in.

Ingredients
  • Water
  • Chamomile
  • Verbena
  • Hemlock
  • Amaranth
  • Juniper
  1. Pick all herbs fresh
  2. Bless your water under a new moon(a full moon has restorative properties)
  3. Take your herbs, drag from North to South across the ground
  4. Wash herbs in blessed water
  5. Make an herb sachet
  6. Steep sachet in blessed New Moon water to make a tea
  7. administer furtively
